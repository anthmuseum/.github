## Welcome!

The [Anthropology Museum at the University of Winnipeg] is dedicated to innovative, community-engaged, and ethically-sound practices for the interpretation of material and intangible cultural heritage. 

This online space, part of our Experimental Museum project, supports experiential co-learning and co-design research. 

[Anthropology Museum at the University of Winnipeg]: https://uwinnipeg.ca/anthropology-museum
